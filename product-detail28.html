Product detail


What it is:
An award-winning eyelash curler with an edge-free design that prevents pinching for an eye-opening, glamorous curl.
Ingredient Callouts:
Free of parabens, formaldehydes, formaldehyde-releasing agents, phthalates, mineral oil, retinyl palmitate, oxybenzone, coal tar, hydroquinone, sulfates SLS & SLES, triclocarban, triclosan, and contains less than one percent synthetic fragrance.
How to use:
- Open the curler and position it at the base of your lashes.
- Gently squeeze down on lashes.
- For the fullest lashes, move the curler up away from the base and squeeze again in the middle of the lash.
- Press one more time close to the tip of the lashes.
- Use a tissue or dry cloth to wipe the eyelash curler and silicone pads clean between uses.
