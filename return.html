RETURNS AND EXCHANGES POLICY

 

Returns and exchanges are subject to the terms below.

 

 

We are glad to exchange or refund any full-price, unsued within 14 days of receipt of your online order. All sales are final after 14 days of receipt.

All items that are returned for an exchange or refund must be unused and have their original packaging.

Itsme Cosmetics is not responsible for return shipping costs or duty reimbursement in the case of international purchases.

Please contact itsmecosmetics@gmail.com for more information regarding returns.

Your return will be processed as soon as possible, but allow up to 7 days for your refund to be processed.

We hold the right to deny refunds if the merchandise returned does not meet our return policy requirements.
