Product detail


What it is: A tool that lifts away dirt and oil with a one-minute cleansing routine and a targeted firming facial massage that leaves skin looking smoother and more youthful.
How to use:
- To unlock LUNA 3, pair with the FOREO For You app. Once paired and settings decided, device can be used without the app.
- Remove all makeup, dampen skin, and apply your favorite cleanser.
- Press the universal button to turn on the LUNA 3.
- Cleanse using circular movements on cheeks and forehead, gliding up and down the nose.
- Press and hold the button for three seconds to turn off device.
- Rinse and pat your face dry. Apply other skincare products.
- Follow the instructions on the FOREO app to activate the firming massage function.
