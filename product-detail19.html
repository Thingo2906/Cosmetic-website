Product detail


What it is: A multipurpose concealer that brightens, corrects, and perfects for up to 16 hours with a creamy medium-to-buildable coverage and natural, radiant finish.
Coverage: Medium
Finish: Radiant
Formulation: Liquid
Highlighted Ingredients:
- Mineral-Tone Balancing Powder: Works to optically correct, not mask, imperfections.
- Light-Diffusing Mineral Powder: Blurs the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles and helps even out skin tone.
- Multi-Active Botanical Blend (Magnolia Bark Extract, Grape Seed Extract, and Vitamin E): Hydrates, firms, reduces redness, and helps strengthen the skin barrier.
How to use:
- Can be applied directly from the applicator, with fingertips, or with the #10 Radiant Creamy Concealer Brush (sold separately).
- To use as a highlighter, select one to three shades lighter than your concealer shade and place on high points of the face.
- To add contour and dimension, apply concealer in the hollows of the cheeks, on temples, and along the hair and jaw lines.
