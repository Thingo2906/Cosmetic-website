What it is: An intensely hydrating serum to target eight signs of aging and quickly tighten skin with breakthrough TET8™ patent, hyaluronic acid, and niacinamide.
Skin Type: Normal, Dry
Skincare Concerns: Dark Spots, Fine Lines and Wrinkles, Loss of Firmness and Elasticity
Highlighted Ingredients:
- TET8™ Patent: A revolutionary technology clinically shown to target all eight signs of aging.
- Instant Tightening Sugars from Honey Locust: Tightens in three minutes and for up to six hours.
- Micro and Macro Hyaluronic Acid and Niacinamide: Two molecular sizes of hyaluronic acid to replenish moisture and visibly plump skin.
